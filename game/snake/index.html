Snake Pit Applet

Instructions
Wait for the applet and sounds to load, then click on the applet area and press the 'S' key to start.

Use the cursor keys to maneuver the snake around the maze. Gobble up the mice to score points but avoid running into the walls or yourself.

When the red key appears, grab it to advance to the next level. If you make it through all five levels, you start back at the beginning but the game will move a little faster.

You begin with three lives, a bonus life is awarded for every 500 points scored.
